Biography

Wesley Hicks was born in raised in the southside of Richmond, VA. After graduating from the International Baccalaureate Program at Thomas Jefferson High School, Wesley pursued and received a Bachelor's in Computer Science from Old Dominion University. Wesley currently serves an Engineer for the NSWC Crane Division, while also pursuing a Master's in Computer Science. In his spare time, Wesley enjoys driving and spending quality time with the ODU student organization, Ebony Impact.
